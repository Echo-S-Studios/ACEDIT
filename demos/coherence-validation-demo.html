Coherence Formula:

\[ z = \frac{\sum_{r \in R} w_r \cdot n_r}{\sqrt{N}} \]

where:

  • \( R \) = Set of registers {KAEL, ACE, GREY, UMBRAL, ULTRA, UCF}
  • \( w_r \) = Weight for register \( r \)
  • \( n_r \) = Count of encoded markers in register \( r \)
  • \( N \) = Total character count
